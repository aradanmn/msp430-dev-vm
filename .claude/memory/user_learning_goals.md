Scott wants to learn embedded systems design at the "design from scratch" level — not just "follow a tutorial and fill in register values." The course builds toward a Tetris handheld gaming device, but the learning is the primary goal.

He identified that the original exercises were too recipe-oriented (copying register configs from tutorials). He chose to restart from Lesson 01 with a fundamentally different approach rather than continue with diminishing returns.

Values: understanding over speed, genuine problem-solving over completion, building things himself over being handed code.
